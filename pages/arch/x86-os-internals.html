CPUID.EAX=0x1

EAX - Version Information (Type, Family, Model, Stepping)

Enter the value returned in EAX after executing CPUID with EAX=0x1. This will decode the processor family, model, stepping, and identify the Intel codename and product.

Example: 0x806ec (Coffee Lake), 0x906ea (Skylake), 0xa0671 (Ice Lake)
